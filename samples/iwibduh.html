Iwibduh

Overview

Iwibduh is located under an obsidion plain. Some areas of it are unbearably cold. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Sprites. Krystyna Tapia The Vain, a Gladiator is here. The Sprites have been charmed by Krystyna Tapia The Vain. She is trying to research Eiieohd Dum.

Artifact

Eiieohd Dum

Eiieohd Dum is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard sphere. Cacophony glows towards it. It smells like cocoa. It is a light white color. When smelled it becomes a force of destiny.
