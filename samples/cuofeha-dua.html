Cuofeha Dua

Overview

Cuofeha Dua is located on a crystal mountain. Parts of Cuofeha Dua are flooded. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Githyanki. Mickie Mccain The Cynical, a Sahuagin Baron is here. The Githyanki worship Mickie Mccain The Cynical. She is trying to find Bedmabedaeci.

Artifact

Bedmabedaeci

Bedmabedaeci is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent figurine. It is a shifting brown color. It smells like dried apple. Cacophony slides towards it. When worn it levitates those nearby.
