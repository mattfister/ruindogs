Ruin Dogs

Guud Ecdaeuaedwi

Overview

Guud Ecdaeuaedwi is located in a spikey mountain. Some areas of it are somewhat hot. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Centaurs. Neal Ma The Callous, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Centaurs are the slaves of Neal Ma The Callous. He is trying to discover The Part-Time Lung.

Artifact

The Part-Time Lung

The Part-Time Lung looks like a hard amulet. Light slips towards it. It smells like rubber. When cradled it becomes a shielding force.
