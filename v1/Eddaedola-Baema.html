Overview

Eddaedola Baema is located under a cursed city. Parts of it are flooded. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Drows. Trista Ramsay The Tactless, a Bulette is here. The Drows are ruled by Trista Ramsay The Tactless. She is trying to research The Good Pleasure.

Artifact

The Good Pleasure

The Good Pleasure has the form of a warm orb. When carried it grants power to its owner.
