The Distant Individual

Overview

The Distant Individual is located on a crystal city. Regions of it are frozen. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Kenku. Kenny Gregg The Interfering, a Myconid Sprout is here. The Kenku worship Kenny Gregg The Interfering. He is trying to find The Conscious Jurisdiction.

Artifact

The Conscious Jurisdiction

The Conscious Jurisdiction is a powerful artifact in the shape of a glassy prism. Fire incinerates around it. It smells like cypress. When cradled it destroys itself.
