The Rotten Feedback

Overview

The Rotten Feedback is located in a haunted rift. Regions of The Rotten Feedback are inaccessible. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Centaurs. Hyacinth Heller The Quick-Tempered, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Centaurs are ruled by Hyacinth Heller The Quick-Tempered. She is trying to recover Gefbaf.

Artifact

Gefbaf

Gefbaf is a powerful artifact in the shape of a mushy rock. It is a shifting gray color. Magic flows near it. It smells like eggs. When thrown it aids memory.
