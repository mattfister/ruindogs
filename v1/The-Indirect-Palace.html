Overview

The Indirect Palace is constructed on a broken plain. Some areas of The Indirect Palace are flooded. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Orc. Debbi Spivey The Dishonest, a Myconid Sovereign is here. The Orc are the minions of Debbi Spivey The Dishonest. She is trying to destroy Caelmawi Laeebadaf.

Artifact

Caelmawi Laeebadaf

Caelmawi Laeebadaf is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard orb. Magic bends around it. When rubbed it dissappears.
