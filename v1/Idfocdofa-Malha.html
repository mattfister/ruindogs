Overview

Idfocdofa Malha is located in a giant city. Parts of it are incredibly hot. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Sahuagin. Dominque Phelan The Inconsiderate, a Succubus is here. The Sahuagin are the soldiers of Dominque Phelan The Inconsiderate. She is trying to find Femha Uaeiaeum.

Femha Uaeiaeum looks like a smooth meteorite. It smells like roasted chicken. It is a shifting red color. When eaten it becomes a shielding force.
