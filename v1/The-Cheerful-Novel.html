Overview

The Cheerful Novel is located under a flooded rift. Regions of it are corrupted. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Darin Thorn The Tactless, a Bearded Devil is here. The Thri-Kreens are ruled by Darin Thorn The Tactless. He is founding a new religion.

Lodwi Faehmd looks like a soft cube. It smells like solvent. It is a sickly brown color. When touched it curses all nearby.
