Hewiuod

Overview

Hewiuod is located under an alien rift. Regions of Hewiuod are corrupted. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Satyrs. Arthur Leger The Careless, a Hobgoblin Captain is here. The Satyrs worship Arthur Leger The Careless. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Defdaecha

Defdaecha is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent figurine. Cacophony shifts near it. When worshipped it grants a wish.
