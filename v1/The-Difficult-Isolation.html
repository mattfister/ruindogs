Overview

The Difficult Isolation is located in a flooded city. Some rooms of it are cursed. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Troglodytes. Mayme Honeycutt The Flirtatious, a Kuo-Toa Whip is here. The Troglodytes are the minions of Mayme Honeycutt The Flirtatious. She is trying to research Cmaed.

Artifact

Cmaed

Cmaed has the form of a hard spear. It is a sickly gray color. It smells like strawberry. Air pours near it. When thrown it becomes a shielding force.
