Overview

Dmamubamum is located in a crystal mountain. Some rooms of Dmamubamum are cursed. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Kuo-Toa. Dale Stillman The Dishonest, a Drow Mage is here. The Kuo-Toa have been charmed by Dale Stillman The Dishonest. He is trying to recover The Childish Rate.

The Childish Rate looks like a cold prism. Psychic energy bends from it. When eaten it makes its owner invisible.
