Overview

Hellaf is located under a flooded plain. Some areas of it are unbearably hot. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Sahuagins. Andre Fortner The Sneaky, a Kuo-Toa Archpriest is here. The Sahuagins are ruled by Andre Fortner The Sneaky. He is trying to steal Aemdfulocdaeum.

Artifact

Aemdfulocdaeum

Aemdfulocdaeum is a powerful artifact in the shape of a glassy gem. It is a medium green color. When held it destroys itself.
