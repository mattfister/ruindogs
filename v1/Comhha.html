Overview

Comhha is located on a cursed rift. Some rooms of it are flooded. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Sahuagin. Hollis Hutchinson The Pusillanimous, a Myconid Sprout is here. The Sahuagin have been charmed by Hollis Hutchinson The Pusillanimous. He is trying to destroy The Dirty Maze.

Artifact

The Dirty Maze

The Dirty Maze looks like a soft orb. It is a dark gray color. When cradled it become energized with a powerful vibration.
