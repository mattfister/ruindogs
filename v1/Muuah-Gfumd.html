Overview

Muuah Gfumd is located under a flooded plain. Some areas of it are frozen. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Humans. Horacio Goss The Self-Centred, a Fire Giant is here. The Humans are the soldiers of Horacio Goss The Self-Centred. He is trying to hide The Quantitative Bronze.

The Quantitative Bronze looks like a smooth cube. It smells like molasses. When held it destroys itself.
