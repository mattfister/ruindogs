Overview

The Private Hunting is located in a ruined mountain. Some rooms of The Private Hunting are flooded. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Quaggoths. Blythe Blackwell The Nervous, a Fire Giant is here. The Quaggoths are the soldiers of Blythe Blackwell The Nervous. She is trying to hide The Formal Sensitivity.

Artifact

The Formal Sensitivity

The Formal Sensitivity is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard prism. It smells like freshly cut hay. It is a light red color. When worn it floats in the air.
