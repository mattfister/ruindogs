Dubedu

Overview

Dubedu is located in a volcanic plain. Some areas of Dubedu are corrupted. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Deep Gnomes. Jutta Pike The Superficial, a Hobgoblin Captain is here. The Deep Gnomes are the slaves of Jutta Pike The Superficial. She is trying to exploit The Fat Ant.

Artifact

The Fat Ant

The Fat Ant is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque cube. Air pours around it. When thrown it becomes a force of destiny.
