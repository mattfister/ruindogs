The Fair Mountain

Overview

The Fair Mountain is located under a broken rift. Regions of it are frozen. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Pixies. Ashton Haskell The Truculent, a Hobgoblin Warlord is here. The Pixies are the soldiers of Ashton Haskell The Truculent. She is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Uuaeca

Uuaeca is a powerful artifact in the shape of a mushy sphere. It smells like buttery cabbage. Fire slips away from it. When carried it frightens children.
