The Corporate Feather

Overview

The Corporate Feather is located under a cursed city. Parts of The Corporate Feather are frozen. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Sahuagin. Sandi Paine The Cruel, a Hill Giant is here. The Sahuagin are ruled by Sandi Paine The Cruel. She is trying to recover The Proper Bail.

Artifact

The Proper Bail

The Proper Bail has the form of an opaque meteorite. Gravity pours towards it. When gazed upon it illuminates its surroundings.
