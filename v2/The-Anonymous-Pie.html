Overview

The Anonymous Pie is located under a flooded tree. Some areas of it are cursed. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Giants. Winfred Morrill The Clinging, a Drow Mage is here. The Giants are the minions of Winfred Morrill The Clinging. He is trying to use The Skilled Secret.

Artifact

The Skilled Secret

The Skilled Secret is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent meteorite. It smells like starfruit. It is a dark orange color. When picked up it levitates surrounding objects.
