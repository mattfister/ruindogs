Overview

Icoefa is located in a crystal rift. Parts of Icoefa are cursed. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Harpies. Virgie Guidry The Secretive, a Myconid Sovereign is here. The Harpies are ruled by Virgie Guidry The Secretive. She is trying to understand Cfuii Daebadedha.

Artifact

Cfuii Daebadedha

Cfuii Daebadedha is a powerful artifact in the shape of a mushy figurine. Fire flows near it. When thrown it grants a wish.
