The Comfortable Cluster

Overview

The Comfortable Cluster is located in a poisoned mountain. Some rooms of it are somewhat cold. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Orcs. Randall Merriman The Selfish, a Cambion is here. The Orcs worship Randall Merriman The Selfish. He is trying to hide Ascaddaeum Ohcaf.

Artifact

Ascaddaeum Ohcaf

Ascaddaeum Ohcaf has the form of a glassy sphere. It is a pale purple color. Light slips near it. When worshipped it becomes a shielding force.
