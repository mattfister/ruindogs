Overview

Mobdaf is located in a flooded city. Some rooms of it are foggy. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Kenku. Lloyd Hancock The Indiscreet, an Orc Eye of Gruumsh is here. The Kenku are battling Lloyd Hancock The Indiscreet. He is trying to discover Deidah Luodd.

Deidah Luodd has the form of a wet spear. It smells like tagette. When smelled it grants a wish.
