Iodafuaeiuf

Overview

Iodafuaeiuf is located on a cursed plain. Some areas of Iodafuaeiuf are somewhat hot. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Troglodytes. Camille Davies The Resentful, a Kuo-Toa Monitor is here. The Troglodytes are battling Camille Davies The Resentful. She is trying to destroy Meaefcod.

Artifact

Meaefcod

Meaefcod is a powerful artifact in the shape of a broken blade. Air flows near it. It smells like starfruit. When thrown it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
