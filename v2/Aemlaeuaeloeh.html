Overview

Aemlaeuaeloeh is located on a haunted city. Some areas of it are frozen. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Orcs. Emile Finney The Gullible, a Gnoll Pack Lord is here. The Orcs are the soldiers of Emile Finney The Gullible. He is trying to discover The Enjoyable Multimedia.

Artifact

The Enjoyable Multimedia

The Enjoyable Multimedia has the form of a sharp gem. It smells like sassafrass. Psychic energy incinerates away from it. When smelled it grants a wish.
