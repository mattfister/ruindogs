Overview

Asdfaiiaeum is located on a ruined city. Parts of it are unbearably hot. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Troglodytes. Beau Dinkins The Belligerent, an Orc War Chief is here. The Troglodytes have been charmed by Beau Dinkins The Belligerent. He is trying to use Boicha.

Boicha

Boicha has the form of a glassy figurine. It is a dark pink color. It smells like coriander. When rubbed it burns the mind.
