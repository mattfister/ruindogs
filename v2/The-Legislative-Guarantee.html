The Legislative Guarantee

Overview

The Legislative Guarantee is constructed on a ruined mountain. Some rooms of it are inaccessible. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Vampires. Bertha Calvin The Indecisive, a Cloud Giant is here. The Vampires have been charmed by Bertha Calvin The Indecisive. She is trying to use Gaefagaehmdaf.

Gaefagaehmdaf

Gaefagaehmdaf looks like a warm gem. It smells like resin. Power glows near it. It is a sickly orange color. When rubbed it destroys itself.
