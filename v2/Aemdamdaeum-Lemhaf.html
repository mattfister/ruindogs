Overview

Aemdamdaeum Lemhaf is located on a broken plain. Regions of it are foggy. The ruin is burning. It is occupied by Dryads. Milda Hilton The Grumpy, an Orc War Chief is here. The Dryads are battling Milda Hilton The Grumpy. She is trying to use Muom Aemlaeuaeloeh.

Muom Aemlaeuaeloeh has the form of an opaque cube. It is a medium orange color. It smells like seashore. When gazed upon it liquifies.
