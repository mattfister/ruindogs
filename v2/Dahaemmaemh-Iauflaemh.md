##Dahaemmaemh Iauflaemh
###Overview
Dahaemmaemh Iauflaemh is located in a crystal rift. Parts of it are frozen. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Giants. <a name="Jarod-Hartley-The-Foolhardy"></a>Jarod Hartley The Foolhardy, a Drow Priestess of Lolth is here. The Giants are the minions of Jarod Hartley The Foolhardy. He  is trying to research [Medofa](#Medofa). 



###Artifact
####<a name="Medofa"></a>Medofa


Medofa has the form of a soft gem. It smells like ginseng. When picked up it tunnels into the earth.
