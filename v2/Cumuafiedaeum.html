Cumuafiedaeum

Overview

Cumuafiedaeum is located on a ruined city. Some areas of Cumuafiedaeum are flooded. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Goblins. Lorriane Weber The Cunning, a Gnoll Pack Lord is here. The Goblins worship Lorriane Weber The Cunning. She is trying to understand The Light Switch.

Artifact

The Light Switch

The Light Switch looks like an opaque prism. It smells like oatmeal. When eaten it aids memory.
