Overview

The Comparable Yacht is located on a flooded city. Regions of The Comparable Yacht are cursed. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Quaggoths. Pierre Razo The Nervous, a Cloud Giant is here. The Quaggoths are the soldiers of Pierre Razo The Nervous. He is trying to steal The Single Sensation.
