Iebdha Mufaesum

Overview

Iebdha Mufaesum is located on a poisoned rift. Regions of it are inaccessible. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Githyanki. Shaunte Elizondo The Thoughtless, a Fire Giant is here. The Githyanki are the minions of Shaunte Elizondo The Thoughtless. She is trying to discover Lacfaeia Ihed.

Artifact

Lacfaeia Ihed

Lacfaeia Ihed has the form of a sharp blade. Magic bends towards it. It is a sickly green color. When carried it becomes lost.
