Overview

Mofiafwi is constructed on an alien mountain. Some rooms of it are cursed. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Goblins. Collin Perron The Clinging, a Fire Giant is here. The Goblins have been charmed by Collin Perron The Clinging. He is trying to exploit Aelae Mahd.

Artifact

Aelae Mahd

Aelae Mahd has the form of a sharp blade. It smells like foliage. When worshipped it floats in the air.
