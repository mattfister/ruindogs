The Subsequent Fraction

Overview

The Subsequent Fraction is constructed on a volcanic plain. Some areas of it are flooded. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Doyle Voss The Inconsistent, an Incubus is here. The Githzerai are the minions of Doyle Voss The Inconsistent. He is trying to discover Haea.

Artifact

Haea

Haea is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent gem. It smells like juice. Light bends around it. When rubbed it floats above the ground.
