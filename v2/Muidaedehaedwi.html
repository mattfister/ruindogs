Muidaedehaedwi

Overview

Muidaedehaedwi is constructed on a haunted city. Regions of it are frozen. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Sahuagins. Ann Crowell The Gullible, a Hobgoblin Warlord is here. The Sahuagins have been charmed by Ann Crowell The Gullible. She is trying to destroy The Strong Inflation.

Artifact

The Strong Inflation

The Strong Inflation looks like a glassy monument. It smells like turmeric. It is a sickly gray color. When worn it tunnels into the earth.
