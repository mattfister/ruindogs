Overview

Eccobohedaeum is located in a crystal city. Some rooms of it are somewhat hot. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Myconids. Mauro Erdmann The Stubborn, a Gladiator is here. The Myconids worship Mauro Erdmann The Stubborn. He is trying to understand The Revolutionary Pavement.
