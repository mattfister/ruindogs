Overview

Faiaelamca Buuabamd is constructed on a cursed tree. Regions of it are cursed. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Dryads. Hugh Fuchs The Belligerent, an Orc Eye of Gruumsh is here. The Dryads are the soldiers of Hugh Fuchs The Belligerent. He is trying to steal The Religious Prison.

The Religious Prison

The Religious Prison looks like a mushy prism. It smells like roasted barley. Fire incinerates near it. When touched it glows with an eerie light.
