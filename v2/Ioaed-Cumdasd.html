Overview

Ioaed Cumdasd is located under an alien rift. Regions of it are cursed. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Deep Gnomes. Beth Ridley The Mean, a Mind Flayer is here. The Deep Gnomes worship Beth Ridley The Mean. She is trying to steal Eiiofemca.

Eiiofemca has the form of a warm cube. It is a medium orange color. When worn it illuminates its surroundings.
