Overview

Fuud Aemghedaeum is located under an alien plain. Some rooms of Fuud Aemghedaeum are foggy. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Kuo-Toa. Trent Shipp The Touchy, a Fire Giant is here. The Kuo-Toa are the minions of Trent Shipp The Touchy. He is trying to discover Hamdhabem.

Artifact

Hamdhabem

Hamdhabem is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard meteorite. It smells like paper. When cradled it floats in the air.
