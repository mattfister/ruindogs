The Holy Time

Overview

The Holy Time is located under a ruined city. Some rooms of The Holy Time are incredibly cold. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Sprites. Emilia Slade The Superficial, a Hill Giant is here. The Sprites are ruled by Emilia Slade The Superficial. She is trying to use Iaddhabamd.

Artifact

Iaddhabamd

Iaddhabamd is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard doll. Water flows around it. It smells like fungus. It is a medium yellow color. When picked up it grants a wish.
