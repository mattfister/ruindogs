Ruin Dogs

Ioccaii Ceddha

Overview

Ioccaii Ceddha is located under a ruined city. Some rooms of it are corrupted. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Drows. Huey Shephard The Indiscreet, an Assassin is here. The Drows have been charmed by Huey Shephard The Indiscreet. He is trying to find Asceuedaeum.

Artifact

Asceuedaeum

Asceuedaeum has the form of a cold gem. It smells like roasted peanut. When eaten it floats in the air.
