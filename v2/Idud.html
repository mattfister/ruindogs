Overview

Idud is located under a haunted mountain. Some areas of it are corrupted. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Centaurs. Morton Skaggs The Inflexible, a Stone Giant is here. The Centaurs are the minions of Morton Skaggs The Inflexible. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Geba

Geba is a powerful artifact in the shape of a sharp meteorite. It is a dark gray color. Light bends away from it. When picked up it becomes a force of destiny.
