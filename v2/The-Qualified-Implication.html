Overview

The Qualified Implication is located in a poisoned rift. Some rooms of The Qualified Implication are incredibly cold. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Kobolds. Ethelene Sallee The Narrow-Minded, an Incubus is here. The Kobolds are ruled by Ethelene Sallee The Narrow-Minded. She is trying to hide Ehobaemaeob.

Artifact

Ehobaemaeob

Ehobaemaeob is a powerful artifact in the shape of a cold gem. It smells like bell pepper. Light flows near it. It is a dark pink color. When smelled it become energized with a powerful vibration.
