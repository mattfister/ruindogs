Coudedaeum

Overview

Coudedaeum is located under a giant rift. Parts of Coudedaeum are incredibly cold. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Harpies. Tara Vail The Ruthless, a Bandit Captain is here. The Harpies have been charmed by Tara Vail The Ruthless. She is trying to discover The Even Fountain.

Artifact

The Even Fountain

The Even Fountain is a powerful artifact in the shape of a warm crystal. When thrown it destroys itself.
