Overview

Cumidfeaemd is constructed on an obsidion plain. Some areas of Cumidfeaemd are foggy. The ruin is burning. It is occupied by Ogres. Kathryn Staton The Indecisive, a Veteran is here. The Ogres have been charmed by Kathryn Staton The Indecisive. She is trying to recover Hhuub Ihaela.

Artifact

Hhuub Ihaela

Hhuub Ihaela is a powerful artifact in the shape of a wet orb. Water glows from it. It smells like plastic. When cradled it becomes a shielding force.
