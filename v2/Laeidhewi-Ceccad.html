Ruin Dogs

Laeidhewi Ceccad

Overview

Laeidhewi Ceccad is constructed on a haunted rift. Regions of Laeidhewi Ceccad are flooded. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Newton Lim The Vague, a Fire Giant is here. The Thri-Kreens are the soldiers of Newton Lim The Vague. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Quiet Distance

The Quiet Distance has the form of a transparent spear. Air slides away from it. It smells like hay. When thrown it tunnels into the earth.
