Overview

The Equal Rain is located under a volcanic city. Some areas of The Equal Rain are cursed. The ruin is burning. It is occupied by Troglodytes. Ethelene Royer The Gullible, a Mind Flayer is here. The Troglodytes are ruled by Ethelene Royer The Gullible. She is founding a new religion.
