Ruin Dogs

Hewiaf

Overview

Hewiaf is constructed on a spikey plain. Some areas of it are unbearably hot. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Antonio Mackie The Belligerent, a Bandit Captain is here. The Thri-Kreens are the slaves of Antonio Mackie The Belligerent. He is trying to recover The Superior Trouble.

Artifact

The Superior Trouble

The Superior Trouble has the form of a transparent cube. Gravity incinerates towards it. When held it burns the mind.
