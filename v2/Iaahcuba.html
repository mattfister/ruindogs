Iaahcuba is located in a haunted city. Some rooms of Iaahcuba are frozen. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Satyrs. Valeri Tiller The Belligerent, a Hill Giant is here. The Satyrs are battling Valeri Tiller The Belligerent. She is trying to hide The Statistical Wheat.

The Statistical Wheat is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque doll. Light pours around it. When touched it emits dust.
