Overview

Aemiaela Gecaehaedwi is located in a giant rift. Regions of Aemiaela Gecaehaedwi are foggy. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Satyrs. Lavonna Waite The Vulgar, a Quaggoth Thonot is here. The Satyrs have been charmed by Lavonna Waite The Vulgar. She is trying to recover The Portable Routine.

Artifact

The Portable Routine

The Portable Routine looks like a hard monument. Power bends away from it. When picked up it levitates those nearby.
