Dafb

Overview

Dafb is located under a haunted plain. Some areas of it are flooded. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Demons. Janice Mcbride The Careless, a Stone Giant is here. The Demons are ruled by Janice Mcbride The Careless. She is trying to discover Imua.

Artifact

Imua

Imua looks like a broken sphere. It smells like onion. Light pours near it. When carried it grants power to its owner.
