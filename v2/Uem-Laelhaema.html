Overview

Uem Laelhaema is located on a crystal rift. Regions of Uem Laelhaema are foggy. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Goblins. Ilda Pina The Naughty, a Kuo-Toa Whip is here. The Goblins are the slaves of Ilda Pina The Naughty. She is trying to destroy Eiwihob.

Artifact

Eiwihob

Eiwihob is a powerful artifact in the shape of a broken sphere. Gravity flows away from it. When held it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
