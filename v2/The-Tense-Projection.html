The Tense Projection

Overview

The Tense Projection is located under a flooded tree. Parts of The Tense Projection are frozen. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Deep Gnomes. Danial Conn The Pompous, a Gnoll Fang of Yeenoghu is here. The Deep Gnomes are battling Danial Conn The Pompous. He is trying to recover Faiaelamd.

Artifact

Faiaelamd

Faiaelamd looks like an opaque monument. Power flows around it. When thrown it becomes lost.
