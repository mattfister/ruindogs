Ladeda Laeuufca

Overview

Ladeda Laeuufca is located under a broken mountain. Some areas of Ladeda Laeuufca are inaccessible. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Drows. Bob Lantz The Intolerant, a Fire Giant is here. The Drows are the minions of Bob Lantz The Intolerant. He is trying to destroy Dfudufdaeum.

Artifact

Dfudufdaeum

Dfudufdaeum looks like a broken monument. It is a shifting gray color. Water shifts near it. When rubbed it becomes hot.
