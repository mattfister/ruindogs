Overview

The Musical Hair is located on a poisoned rift. Parts of The Musical Hair are somewhat hot. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Gnolls. Ila Boggs The Intolerant, a Fire Giant is here. The Gnolls are the slaves of Ila Boggs The Intolerant. She is trying to discover Mahudaeedaeum Mod.

Mahudaeedaeum Mod looks like a wet crystal. Fire glows away from it. It is a dark yellow color. It smells like camphoreous. When worshipped it repels insects.
