##Dufdofa
###Overview
Dufdofa is located in a giant rift. Regions of Dufdofa are flooded. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Lizardfolk. <a name="Honey-Herman-The-Finicky"></a>Honey Herman The Finicky, a Bulette is here. The Lizardfolk are battling Honey Herman The Finicky. She  is founding a new religion. 



###Artifact
####<a name="Laefacdaeum"></a>Laefacdaeum


Laefacdaeum looks like a transparent crystal. When cradled it become energized with a powerful vibration.
