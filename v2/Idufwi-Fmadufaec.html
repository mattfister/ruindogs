Idufwi Fmadufaec

Overview

Idufwi Fmadufaec is located under an alien rift. Parts of it are frozen. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Myconids. Emerita Bergstrom The Belligerent, a Fire Giant is here. The Myconids worship Emerita Bergstrom The Belligerent. She is trying to find The Illegal Record.

Artifact

The Illegal Record

The Illegal Record has the form of a wet crystal. Power slides towards it. When gazed upon it grants power to its owner.
