Dfaiaelamd

Overview

Dfaiaelamd is located under a volcanic mountain. Some rooms of it are foggy. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Pearle Begley The Quick-Tempered, a Bulette is here. The Thri-Kreens have been charmed by Pearle Begley The Quick-Tempered. She is trying to understand The Compatible Pension.

Artifact

The Compatible Pension

The Compatible Pension has the form of a wet spear. When rubbed it curses all nearby.
