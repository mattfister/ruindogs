Cumcadd

Overview

Cumcadd is located under a haunted rift. Some rooms of Cumcadd are frozen. The ruin is burning. It is occupied by Sprites. Britta Mccallum The Greedy, a Quaggoth is here. The Sprites are the minions of Britta Mccallum The Greedy. She is trying to find Iohef.

Artifact

Iohef

Iohef is a powerful artifact in the shape of a cold doll. It smells like citronella. It is a sickly gray color. When cradled it projects energy.
