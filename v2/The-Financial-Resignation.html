The Financial Resignation

Overview

The Financial Resignation is located under a haunted mountain. Some rooms of The Financial Resignation are unbearably hot. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Troglodytes. Clorinda Schaffer The Ruthless, a Fire Giant is here. The Troglodytes are the minions of Clorinda Schaffer The Ruthless. She is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Broken Orange

The Broken Orange is a powerful artifact in the shape of a cold doll. It smells like peppermint. It is a medium pink color. When held it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
