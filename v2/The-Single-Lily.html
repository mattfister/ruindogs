Overview

The Single Lily is located under a cursed mountain. Regions of The Single Lily are somewhat hot. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Sprites. Cyndi Burdette The Nasty, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Sprites are the slaves of Cyndi Burdette The Nasty. She is trying to destroy Camioi Gaehofa.

Camioi Gaehofa

Camioi Gaehofa has the form of a glassy cube. Psychic energy pours from it. When gazed upon it floats in the air.
