Overview

Lauaeedaeum Eccaelamd is located in a haunted plain. Parts of it are frozen. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Mind Flayers. Deonna William The Aggressive, a Drow Priestess of Lolth is here. The Mind Flayers are battling Deonna William The Aggressive. She is trying to research Ecdaeum Hehhafwi.

Ecdaeum Hehhafwi has the form of a soft gem. Air glows near it. It smells like brandy. When worn it burns the mind.
