Overview

Fagohaa is located under an obsidion city. Regions of it are inaccessible. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Satyrs. Kristi Ivy The Truculent, a Priest is here. The Satyrs are battling Kristi Ivy The Truculent. She is trying to use Cmemca Omcha.

Artifact

Cmemca Omcha

Cmemca Omcha is a powerful artifact in the shape of a cold figurine. Air slips around it. When worn it becomes lost.
