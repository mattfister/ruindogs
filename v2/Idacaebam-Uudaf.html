Idacaebam Uudaf

Overview

Idacaebam Uudaf is located under a ruined tree. Some areas of it are cursed. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Ogres. Ellen Allman The Impulsive, a Yuan-Ti Abomination is here. The Ogres are the soldiers of Ellen Allman The Impulsive. She is trying to discover Ehumwi.

Artifact

Ehumwi

Ehumwi looks like a wet spear. It is a bright gray color. When picked up it grants a wish.
