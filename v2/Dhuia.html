Overview

Dhuia is located under a volcanic rift. Some areas of Dhuia are cursed. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Satyrs. Jamel Pugh The Truculent, a Sahuagin Baron is here. The Satyrs are the minions of Jamel Pugh The Truculent. He is trying to hide Bafaed Aebdecd.

Bafaed Aebdecd

Bafaed Aebdecd looks like a transparent monument. It smells like root beer. It is a sickly green color. When picked up it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
