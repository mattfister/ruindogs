Overview

Dafb Iugdiaefa is located in a poisoned rift. Some areas of it are flooded. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Sprites. Leann Wilburn The Changeable, a Drow Priestess of Lolth is here. The Sprites have been charmed by Leann Wilburn The Changeable. She is trying to find The Intermediate Bird.

The Intermediate Bird

The Intermediate Bird has the form of a warm cube. Fire incinerates around it. When held it destroys itself.
