The Unemployed Gold

Overview

The Unemployed Gold is located in a spikey rift. Regions of it are corrupted. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Drows. Jolyn Browder The Tactless, a Yuan-Ti Malison is here. The Drows are ruled by Jolyn Browder The Tactless. She is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Ufhem

Ufhem is a powerful artifact in the shape of a broken monument. Psychic energy flows away from it. When carried it burns the mind.
