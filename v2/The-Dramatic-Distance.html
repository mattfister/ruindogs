The Dramatic Distance

Overview

The Dramatic Distance is located under an alien mountain. Some areas of it are foggy. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Pixies. Jessenia Estrada The Changeable, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Pixies are battling Jessenia Estrada The Changeable. She is trying to recover Cofwi Lagaed.

Artifact

Cofwi Lagaed

Cofwi Lagaed is a powerful artifact in the shape of a sharp cube. It smells like mimosa. When smelled it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
