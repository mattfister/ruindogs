Overview

The Urban Strap is constructed on an alien rift. Some areas of The Urban Strap are flooded. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Goblins. Modesto Steffen The Bone-Idle, an Assassin is here. The Goblins are the slaves of Modesto Steffen The Bone-Idle. He is trying to use Muha.

Muha has the form of a glassy prism. It is a bright red color. It smells like tangerine. When carried it destroys others.
