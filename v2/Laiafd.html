Laiafd

Overview

Laiafd is located under a ruined mountain. Some areas of Laiafd are flooded. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Kobolds. Marcelino Cavazos The Domineering, an Incubus is here. The Kobolds have been charmed by Marcelino Cavazos The Domineering. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Retired Embarrassment

The Retired Embarrassment has the form of a glassy crystal. Water slides towards it. When picked up it aids memory.
