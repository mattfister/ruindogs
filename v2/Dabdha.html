Dabdha

Overview

Dabdha is constructed on a flooded tree. Some areas of it are cursed. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Goblins. Kory Champagne The Perverse, a Yuan-Ti Abomination is here. The Goblins are the slaves of Kory Champagne The Perverse. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Imefamuhlaf Imufd

Imefamuhlaf Imufd has the form of an opaque blade. Water shifts near it. It is a shifting yellow color. When carried it changes the past.
