The Sudden Humanity is located under an alien mountain. Regions of The Sudden Humanity are cursed. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Ogres. Fernando Callaghan The Truculent, a Yuan-Ti Malison is here. The Ogres are ruled by Fernando Callaghan The Truculent. He is trying to research The Awkward Link.
