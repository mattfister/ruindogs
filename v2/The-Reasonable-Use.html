The Reasonable Use

Overview

The Reasonable Use is located under a flooded mountain. Some areas of The Reasonable Use are somewhat cold. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Centaurs. Thurman Goode The Possessive, a Mind Flayer is here. The Centaurs are the slaves of Thurman Goode The Possessive. He is trying to find Deiadehh.

Artifact

Deiadehh

Deiadehh has the form of a sharp doll. Water incinerates away from it. When smelled it flies into the air.
