Gfaccha

Overview

Gfaccha is located in a haunted mountain. Some rooms of it are cursed. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Sprites. Darrel Heflin The Sarcastic, a Mind Flayer is here. The Sprites are the slaves of Darrel Heflin The Sarcastic. He is trying to use The Content Survivor.

Artifact

The Content Survivor

The Content Survivor has the form of a smooth blade. Fire incinerates near it. When touched it sings the hymn of the damned.
