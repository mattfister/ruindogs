Overview

The Incredible Border is constructed on a broken city. Some areas of The Incredible Border are foggy. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Sahuagins. Curtis Whittle The Boastful, a Gladiator is here. The Sahuagins worship Curtis Whittle The Boastful. He is trying to understand Mufdm.

Mufdm

Mufdm looks like a mushy orb. It is a bright black color. When smelled it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
