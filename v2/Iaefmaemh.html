Overview

Iaefmaemh is located under a flooded plain. Parts of it are cursed. The ruin is burning. It is occupied by Centaurs. Curtis Delvalle The Grumpy, a Drow Priestess of Lolth is here. The Centaurs are the slaves of Curtis Delvalle The Grumpy. She is trying to find The Continuous Slave.

Artifact

The Continuous Slave

The Continuous Slave is a powerful artifact in the shape of a broken rock. It is a pale purple color. It smells like coffee. Cacophony pours around it. When worn it becomes lost.
