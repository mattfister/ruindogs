Defdmaf is located in a broken city. Regions of it are foggy. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Orcs. Earnestine Mccauley The Bossy, an Assassin is here. The Orcs are the slaves of Earnestine Mccauley The Bossy. She is trying to steal Cumcadd.

Cumcadd is a powerful artifact in the shape of a smooth rock. When cradled it becomes a force of destiny.
