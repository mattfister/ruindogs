Uaeaia Iaeaeid

Overview

Uaeaia Iaeaeid is located under a giant tree. Some areas of it are flooded. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Giants. Katrina Sylvester The Clinging, a Kuo-Toa Archpriest is here. The Giants have been charmed by Katrina Sylvester The Clinging. She is trying to destroy The Distant Statement.

Artifact

The Distant Statement

The Distant Statement is a powerful artifact in the shape of a glassy rock. Magic shifts towards it. It smells like roasted barley. When worshipped it aids memory.
