Overview

Dfeml is constructed on a haunted plain. Some areas of Dfeml are flooded. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Yuan-Ti. Deanne Ouellette The Unpredictable, a Vampire Spawn is here. The Yuan-Ti are ruled by Deanne Ouellette The Unpredictable. She is founding a new religion.
