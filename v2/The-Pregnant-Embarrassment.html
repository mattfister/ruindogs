The Pregnant Embarrassment

Overview

The Pregnant Embarrassment is located in a poisoned mountain. Some areas of it are inaccessible. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Orcs. Rueben Palacios The Aloof, a Fire Giant is here. The Orcs are ruled by Rueben Palacios The Aloof. He is trying to destroy Dfaeium.

Artifact

Dfaeium

Dfaeium is a powerful artifact in the shape of a sharp monument. Cacophony pours away from it. When carried it becomes a shielding force.
