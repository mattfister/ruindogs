The Wet Studio

Overview

The Wet Studio is located on a cursed plain. Some rooms of it are flooded. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Kenku. Tana Mcculloch The Inconsiderate, an Incubus is here. The Kenku are the minions of Tana Mcculloch The Inconsiderate. She is trying to research Dewi Iwibddub.

Artifact

Dewi Iwibddub

Dewi Iwibddub is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque cube. Fire slides near it. It smells like corn. When worshipped it levitates those nearby.
