##Idfamhdm
###Overview
Idfamhdm is located under a broken city. Some rooms of Idfamhdm are incredibly cold. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Duergars. <a name="Alfonso-Roman-The-Nasty"></a>Alfonso Roman The Nasty, a Lizardfolk Shaman is here. The Duergars are the soldiers of Alfonso Roman The Nasty. He  is trying to understand [Aemdahfedaeum Heda](#Aemdahfedaeum-Heda). 



###Artifact
####<a name="Aemdahfedaeum-Heda"></a>Aemdahfedaeum Heda


Aemdahfedaeum Heda is a powerful artifact in the shape of a smooth spear. When worshipped it levitates surrounding objects.
