Cehufaea Laedhubed

Overview

Cehufaea Laedhubed is located in a flooded rift. Parts of Cehufaea Laedhubed are unbearably cold. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Deep Gnomes. Bruno Harness The Lazy, a Drow Mage is here. The Deep Gnomes are battling Bruno Harness The Lazy. He is trying to exploit The Friendly Double.

Artifact

The Friendly Double

The Friendly Double looks like a cold rock. It is a light yellow color. Gravity incinerates from it. It smells like unripe banana. When gazed upon it levitates surrounding objects.
