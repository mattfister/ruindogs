Overview

Gfaimbem Uiamafimaed is located under a spikey city. Some rooms of it are frozen. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Vito Joyner The Secretive, a Fire Giant is here. The Githzerai have been charmed by Vito Joyner The Secretive. He is trying to discover Daffwi.

Artifact

Daffwi

Daffwi looks like a mushy sphere. It smells like cumin. Water shifts away from it. It is a shifting black color. When touched it tunnels into the earth.
