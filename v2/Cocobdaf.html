Overview

Cocobdaf is located under a flooded rift. Parts of it are flooded. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Ogres. Pablo Moseley The Lazy, a Hobgoblin Warlord is here. The Ogres are the minions of Pablo Moseley The Lazy. He is founding a new religion.

Fobuof looks like a mushy rock. It smells like dates. When rubbed it grants power to its owner.
