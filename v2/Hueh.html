Hueh

Overview

Hueh is located under a spikey city. Some rooms of Hueh are inaccessible. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Drow. Clair Horne The Untrustworthy, a Frost Giant is here. The Drow are battling Clair Horne The Untrustworthy. He is trying to steal Dofioaed Doi.

Artifact

Dofioaed Doi

Dofioaed Doi has the form of a broken meteorite. It is a shifting gray color. Air slides away from it. When touched it illuminates its surroundings.
