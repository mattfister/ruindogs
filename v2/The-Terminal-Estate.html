Overview

The Terminal Estate is constructed on a flooded city. Regions of it are unbearably cold. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Orcs. Loreen Royer The Miserly, a Succubus is here. The Orcs worship Loreen Royer The Miserly. She is trying to find Ieoca.

Ieoca looks like a soft meteorite. It is a medium white color. When eaten it aids memory.
