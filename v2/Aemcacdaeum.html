Aemcacdaeum

Overview

Aemcacdaeum is located in a broken city. Regions of it are inaccessible. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Duergars. Sherrill Archuleta The Clinging, a Drow Elite Warrior is here. The Duergars are the slaves of Sherrill Archuleta The Clinging. She is trying to hide Mufuicuda Cmemmah.

Artifact

Mufuicuda Cmemmah

Mufuicuda Cmemmah looks like a sharp figurine. It smells like graham cracker. It is a shifting gray color. Magic glows towards it. When picked up it sings the hymn of the damned.
