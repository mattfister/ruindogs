Overview

Daedha Huhaec is located on an obsidion tree. Regions of it are cursed. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Myconids. Danette Hunt The Arrogant, a Barbed Devil is here. The Myconids are ruled by Danette Hunt The Arrogant. She is founding a new religion.

Muuah

Muuah is a powerful artifact in the shape of a soft spear. It is a light blue color. It smells like melon. When worn it repels insects.
