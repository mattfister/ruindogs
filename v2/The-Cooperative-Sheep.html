The Cooperative Sheep

Overview

The Cooperative Sheep is located in a spikey city. Regions of it are foggy. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Harpies. Bennie Francois The Irresponsible, a Bearded Devil is here. The Harpies are the soldiers of Bennie Francois The Irresponsible. He is trying to understand Haedafedofa.

Artifact

Haedafedofa

Haedafedofa is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque doll. It smells like raw reseda. It is a sickly white color. When eaten it glows with an eerie light.
