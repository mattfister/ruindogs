Buul Baeh

Overview

Buul Baeh is located under a flooded rift. Parts of it are corrupted. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Sahuagin. Kenneth Montague The Perverse, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Sahuagin are the minions of Kenneth Montague The Perverse. He is trying to discover The Optimistic Contrary.

Artifact

The Optimistic Contrary

The Optimistic Contrary looks like a broken meteorite. Light pours around it. It smells like raw potato. When gazed upon it becomes a deadly projectile.
