Overview

Imud is constructed on a giant rift. Parts of it are foggy. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Ogres. Cole Gladney The Inflexible, a Harpy is here. The Ogres worship Cole Gladney The Inflexible. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Conscious Hall

The Conscious Hall is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard blade. It smells like smoked sausage. It is a sickly red color. Magic glows from it. When picked up it levitates surrounding objects.
