Overview

Dus Hobd is located on a spikey city. Some areas of it are somewhat cold. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Deep Gnomes. Setsuko Stubbs The Fussy, a Mind Flayer is here. The Deep Gnomes are battling Setsuko Stubbs The Fussy. She is trying to recover Cehufaea Cfuiiaemh.

Artifact

Cehufaea Cfuiiaemh

Cehufaea Cfuiiaemh is a powerful artifact in the shape of a soft sphere. It is a pale pink color. It smells like balsamic. When carried it changes probabilities.
