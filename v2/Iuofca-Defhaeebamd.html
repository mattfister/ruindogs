Overview

Iuofca Defhaeebamd is located on a spikey rift. Parts of Iuofca Defhaeebamd are unbearably cold. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Cyclopses. Debbra Cosgrove The Resentful, a Cloud Giant is here. The Cyclopses are the slaves of Debbra Cosgrove The Resentful. She is trying to exploit Dfudufdaeum.

Artifact

Dfudufdaeum

Dfudufdaeum has the form of a sharp rock. Air pours near it. When worn it burns the mind.
