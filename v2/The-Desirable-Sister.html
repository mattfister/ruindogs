The Desirable Sister

Overview

The Desirable Sister is located under an obsidion rift. Some rooms of it are foggy. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Lizardfolk. Hai Haugen The Jealous, a Bulette is here. The Lizardfolk are ruled by Hai Haugen The Jealous. He is trying to find Dud.

Artifact

Dud

Dud is a powerful artifact in the shape of a smooth prism. Cacophony bends towards it. It is a medium green color. When worn it makes its owner invisible.
