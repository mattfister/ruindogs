The Necessary Exile

Overview

The Necessary Exile is constructed on a cursed rift. Regions of The Necessary Exile are flooded. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Dong Steel The Sarcastic, a Kuo-Toa Archpriest is here. The Thri-Kreens are the minions of Dong Steel The Sarcastic. He is trying to destroy Asebaemedaeum.

Artifact

Asebaemedaeum

Asebaemedaeum looks like a mushy monument. It smells like unripe banana. When picked up it becomes a shielding force.
