Iaufcimud Asebaemedaeum

Overview

Iaufcimud Asebaemedaeum is located under an obsidion city. Some rooms of it are flooded. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Bobbi Guerrero The Sarcastic, a Myconid Sprout is here. The Thri-Kreens have been charmed by Bobbi Guerrero The Sarcastic. She is trying to research The Fashionable Impulse.

Artifact

The Fashionable Impulse

The Fashionable Impulse has the form of a transparent crystal. It is a pale white color. Gravity incinerates towards it. When cradled it glows with an eerie light.
