Overview

Dfoim is located on an alien tree. Some areas of it are foggy. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Giants. Eldridge Silverman The Cunning, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Giants are battling Eldridge Silverman The Cunning. He is trying to hide Dmafedwi.

Artifact

Dmafedwi

Dmafedwi has the form of an opaque figurine. Psychic energy slides near it. It smells like dried apple. When picked up it aids memory.
