Overview

Baellha Dfuud is located in a ruined plain. Parts of it are cursed. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Dryads. Eula Bear The Greedy, a Fire Giant is here. The Dryads are the soldiers of Eula Bear The Greedy. She is trying to recover Faiaefcmaf Maeuam.

Artifact

Faiaefcmaf Maeuam

Faiaefcmaf Maeuam looks like a transparent sphere. Water slips near it. It is a pale green color. It smells like verbena. When gazed upon it grants power to its owner.
