Dfacalamd

Overview

Dfacalamd is located in a haunted mountain. Regions of it are flooded. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Deep Gnomes. Willian Hollingsworth The Greedy, a Cloud Giant is here. The Deep Gnomes are the minions of Willian Hollingsworth The Greedy. He is trying to find The Senior Form.

Artifact

The Senior Form

The Senior Form is a powerful artifact in the shape of a soft sphere. It is a bright black color. Cacophony slides around it. It smells like autumn. When worn it levitates those nearby.
