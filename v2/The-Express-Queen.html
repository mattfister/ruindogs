Overview

The Express Queen is located on a giant tree. Parts of it are incredibly hot. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Vergie Salcedo The Foolhardy, a Cyclops is here. The Thri-Kreens are ruled by Vergie Salcedo The Foolhardy. She is founding a new religion.

Asebdha

Asebdha is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque figurine. It is a light pink color. Light incinerates away from it. When gazed upon it burns the mind.
