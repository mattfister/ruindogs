Overview

The Charismatic Attention is constructed on a broken plain. Regions of it are flooded. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Eboni Hostetler The Secretive, a Cyclops is here. The Githzerai are the soldiers of Eboni Hostetler The Secretive. She is trying to destroy Mula Dedha.

Mula Dedha is a powerful artifact in the shape of a warm cube. It is a medium purple color. It smells like burnt butter. When held it becomes hot.
