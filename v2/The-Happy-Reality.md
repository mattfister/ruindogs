##The Happy Reality
###Overview
The Happy Reality is located under an obsidion mountain. Regions of it are incredibly hot. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Myconids. <a name="Delmer-Vitale-The-Dogmatic"></a>Delmer Vitale The Dogmatic, a Frost Giant is here. The Myconids are the slaves of Delmer Vitale The Dogmatic. He  is founding a new religion. 



###Artifact
####<a name="The-Dynamic-Relief"></a>The Dynamic Relief


The Dynamic Relief looks like a smooth sphere. It is a dark blue color. When picked up it destroys itself.
