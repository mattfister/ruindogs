Overview

Gfehbamd is located in a ruined plain. Regions of Gfehbamd are somewhat cold. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Sahuagins. Willie Spencer The Superficial, a Kuo-Toa Archpriest is here. The Sahuagins are the slaves of Willie Spencer The Superficial. He is trying to use Baemaeidaf.

Baemaeidaf

Baemaeidaf is a powerful artifact in the shape of a wet meteorite. Fire flows from it. It smells like allspice. It is a dark blue color. When picked up it liquifies.
