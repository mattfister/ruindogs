Overview

Cubdfubaeia Cfaeiaei is located on an alien mountain. Some areas of it are inaccessible. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Ogres. Clementine Lamm The Obsessive, an Orc War Chief is here. The Ogres are ruled by Clementine Lamm The Obsessive. She is trying to exploit The Hard Civilization.

The Hard Civilization has the form of a hard amulet. Light shifts towards it. It is a bright brown color. It smells like balsam petal. When gazed upon it changes probabilities.
