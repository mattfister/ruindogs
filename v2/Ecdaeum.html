Overview

Ecdaeum is located on a flooded plain. Regions of Ecdaeum are unbearably hot. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Sahuagins. Nestor Southard The Quick-Tempered, a Kuo-Toa Archpriest is here. The Sahuagins are ruled by Nestor Southard The Quick-Tempered. He is trying to understand The Harmful Leadership.

Artifact

The Harmful Leadership

The Harmful Leadership has the form of a broken monument. It is a shifting white color. When rubbed it tunnels into the earth.
