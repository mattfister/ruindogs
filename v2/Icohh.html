Overview

Icohh is located in a giant tree. Parts of it are incredibly cold. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Goblins. Omar Bolton The Stupid, a Druid is here. The Goblins are the soldiers of Omar Bolton The Stupid. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Capable Password

The Capable Password has the form of an opaque doll. Gravity flows around it. It is a dark red color. When touched it changes probabilities.
