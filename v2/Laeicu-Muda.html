Laeicu Muda

Overview

Laeicu Muda is constructed on a spikey rift. Regions of it are flooded. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Ogres. Howard Mooney The Pessimistic, a Hobgoblin Captain is here. The Ogres are the soldiers of Howard Mooney The Pessimistic. He is trying to recover Iohhaidaeum Geaefwi.

Artifact

Iohhaidaeum Geaefwi

Iohhaidaeum Geaefwi is a powerful artifact in the shape of a mushy crystal. Power flows from it. When eaten it becomes a deadly projectile.
