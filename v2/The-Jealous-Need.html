The Jealous Need

Overview

The Jealous Need is located under a crystal city. Some areas of it are somewhat cold. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Giants. Darnell Garnett The Untrustworthy, a Drow Mage is here. The Giants are ruled by Darnell Garnett The Untrustworthy. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Written Resignation

The Written Resignation is a powerful artifact in the shape of a broken crystal. Light bends from it. When eaten it repels insects.
