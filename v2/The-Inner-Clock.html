Overview

The Inner Clock is located under a crystal mountain. Some areas of The Inner Clock are frozen. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Ogres. Cassi Dickens The Unkind, a Fire Giant is here. The Ogres are the slaves of Cassi Dickens The Unkind. She is trying to use Chudmai Ioaed.

Chudmai Ioaed looks like a warm meteorite. It smells like naphthyl. It is a shifting black color. When picked up it burns the mind.
