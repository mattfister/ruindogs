The Jealous Prevalence

Overview

The Jealous Prevalence is located under an obsidion mountain. Some rooms of it are corrupted. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Kobolds. Donny Mosier The Boring, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Kobolds are ruled by Donny Mosier The Boring. He is trying to hide The Provincial Memorandum.

Artifact

The Provincial Memorandum

The Provincial Memorandum looks like a smooth doll. Gravity flows from it. It is a dark white color. When eaten it makes its owner invisible.
