The Frozen Drum

Overview

The Frozen Drum is located under an alien city. Regions of it are frozen. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Demons. Hiram Morin The Rude, a Succubus is here. The Demons have been charmed by Hiram Morin The Rude. He is trying to discover Dasdofa Elbaefedaeum.

Artifact

Dasdofa Elbaefedaeum

Dasdofa Elbaefedaeum is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent meteorite. Light slides towards it. When worshipped it curses all nearby.
