Overview

Iacofaedwi is constructed on a giant rift. Some rooms of it are flooded. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Githyanki. Ethel Thornburg The Greedy, a Yuan-Ti Abomination is here. The Githyanki are ruled by Ethel Thornburg The Greedy. She is trying to hide Ihaeca Laeidofdemca.

Artifact

Ihaeca Laeidofdemca

Ihaeca Laeidofdemca has the form of a wet blade. It smells like peach. Water glows around it. It is a medium black color. When rubbed it frightens children.
