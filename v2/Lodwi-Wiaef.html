Overview

Lodwi Wiaef is located on a cursed rift. Regions of it are inaccessible. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Demons. Randy Mcdade The Indiscreet, an Orc Eye of Gruumsh is here. The Demons are battling Randy Mcdade The Indiscreet. He is trying to research Daudha.

Artifact

Daudha

Daudha is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque crystal. It is a light green color. When carried it tunnels into the earth.
