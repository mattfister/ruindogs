Overview

The Important Chimney is located on a poisoned tree. Some rooms of The Important Chimney are foggy. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Duergars. Victor Bonilla The Superficial, a Kuo-Toa Archpriest is here. The Duergars are battling Victor Bonilla The Superficial. He is trying to research The Vacant Jazz.

The Vacant Jazz

The Vacant Jazz is a powerful artifact in the shape of a mushy meteorite. Light pours away from it. It smells like cardamom. It is a medium green color. When picked up it destroys others.
