Overview

Haea is located on a broken tree. Parts of it are corrupted. A solar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Carlos Mcvey The Timid, a Gladiator is here. The Githzerai are ruled by Carlos Mcvey The Timid. He is trying to research Cuhum Muoia.

Cuhum Muoia

Cuhum Muoia has the form of a hard crystal. It smells like herbs. When smelled it becomes a deadly projectile.
