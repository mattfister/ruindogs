Amdaedhabamd Beii

Overview

Amdaedhabamd Beii is constructed on a flooded mountain. Regions of it are unbearably cold. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Kerrie Church The Grumpy, a Hobgoblin Captain is here. The Thri-Kreens are battling Kerrie Church The Grumpy. She is trying to find Cmeaem.

Artifact

Cmeaem

Cmeaem is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent figurine. When rubbed it levitates surrounding objects.
