The Agile Degree

Overview

The Agile Degree is constructed on a poisoned plain. Parts of it are frozen. The ruin is burning. It is occupied by Ogres. Ronny Draper The Cruel, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Ogres have been charmed by Ronny Draper The Cruel. He is trying to steal Iofdfaeia.

Artifact

Iofdfaeia

Iofdfaeia has the form of a broken monument. It smells like cauliflower. When smelled it becomes hot.
