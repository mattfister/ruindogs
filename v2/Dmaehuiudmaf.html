Ruin Dogs

Dmaehuiudmaf

Overview

Dmaehuiudmaf is located under a broken city. Parts of it are flooded. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Centaurs. Sebrina Logue The Untrustworthy, a Veteran is here. The Centaurs worship Sebrina Logue The Untrustworthy. She is trying to find Hoaela.

Artifact

Hoaela

Hoaela is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent blade. When eaten it dissappears.
