Galafedaeum Hagd

Overview

Galafedaeum Hagd is constructed on a haunted rift. Regions of it are corrupted. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Ogres. Gil Barkley The Selfish, a Frost Giant is here. The Ogres are the slaves of Gil Barkley The Selfish. He is trying to discover The Sharp Width.

Artifact

The Sharp Width

The Sharp Width looks like a transparent blade. It smells like yuzu. When thrown it becomes lost.
