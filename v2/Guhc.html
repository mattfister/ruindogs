Guhc

Overview

Guhc is located in a crystal plain. Parts of it are frozen. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Kobolds. Jae Ferguson The Untidy, a Githyanki Warrior is here. The Kobolds have been charmed by Jae Ferguson The Untidy. He is trying to steal The Physical Disease.

Artifact

The Physical Disease

The Physical Disease has the form of an opaque gem. It is a shifting white color. Magic bends near it. When worn it ignites its surrroundings.
