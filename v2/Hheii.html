Overview

Hheii is located under a flooded mountain. Some rooms of Hheii are incredibly hot. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Ogres. Cherie Jacobsen The Untrustworthy, a Stone Giant is here. The Ogres are the slaves of Cherie Jacobsen The Untrustworthy. She is trying to discover Haehmd.

Haehmd

Haehmd has the form of a hard meteorite. Cacophony slips around it. When rubbed it aids memory.
