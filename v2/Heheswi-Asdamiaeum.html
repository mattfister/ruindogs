Overview

Heheswi Asdamiaeum is constructed on a ruined mountain. Regions of Heheswi Asdamiaeum are flooded. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Kenku. Sebastian Partridge The Callous, a Hobgoblin Warlord is here. The Kenku are the slaves of Sebastian Partridge The Callous. He is trying to hide The Inadequate Green.

The Inadequate Green looks like an opaque orb. When gazed upon it floats in the air.
