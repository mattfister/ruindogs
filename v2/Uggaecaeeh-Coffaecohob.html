Overview

Uggaecaeeh Coffaecohob is constructed on a giant city. Regions of Uggaecaeeh Coffaecohob are unbearably cold. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Kobolds. Fernando Mcclinton The Untidy, a Barbed Devil is here. The Kobolds worship Fernando Mcclinton The Untidy. He is trying to discover The Formal Relaxation.

Artifact

The Formal Relaxation

The Formal Relaxation is a powerful artifact in the shape of an opaque prism. It smells like davana. Gravity slides away from it. It is a dark black color. When worn it flies into the air.
