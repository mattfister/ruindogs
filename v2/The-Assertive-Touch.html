The Assertive Touch

Overview

The Assertive Touch is located under a broken plain. Some rooms of it are incredibly hot. A massive flood is happening outside. It is occupied by Humans. Vania Stpierre The Thoughtless, a Drow Mage is here. The Humans are the soldiers of Vania Stpierre The Thoughtless. She is trying to use Iulaeob Defd.

Iulaeob Defd

Iulaeob Defd looks like an opaque prism. It smells like ham. When touched it tunnels into the earth.
