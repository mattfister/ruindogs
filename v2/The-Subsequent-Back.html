Overview

The Subsequent Back is located under a broken city. Some rooms of The Subsequent Back are flooded. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Drows. Rob Billingsley The Lazy, a Cloud Giant is here. The Drows are ruled by Rob Billingsley The Lazy. He is trying to exploit Faeia Ehamle.

Faeia Ehamle has the form of a cold monument. Fire bends near it. It is a shifting red color. It smells like verbena. When worn it destroys itself.
