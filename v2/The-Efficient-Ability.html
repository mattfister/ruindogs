The Efficient Ability is located under a spikey tree. Parts of it are frozen. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Kanisha Calkins The Self-Indulgent, a Cult Fanatic is here. The Githzerai are the slaves of Kanisha Calkins The Self-Indulgent. She is founding a new religion.

Budufaeid Dfaml is a powerful artifact in the shape of a hard orb. It smells like cinnamon. Psychic energy glows around it. When worn it becomes a force of destiny.
