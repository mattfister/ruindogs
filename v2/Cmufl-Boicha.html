Cmufl Boicha

Overview

Cmufl Boicha is located on a haunted mountain. Regions of Cmufl Boicha are cursed. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Ogres. Krystina Way The Sneaky, a Gnoll Fang of Yeenoghu is here. The Ogres are the minions of Krystina Way The Sneaky. She is trying to recover The Important Corn.

Artifact

The Important Corn

The Important Corn has the form of a smooth amulet. It smells like buttery cabbage. Water bends from it. When cradled it levitates those nearby.
