Cfaia

Overview

Cfaia is located under a volcanic tree. Parts of Cfaia are frozen. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Orcs. Darren Meadows The Timid, a Yuan-Ti Abomination is here. The Orcs are the minions of Darren Meadows The Timid. He is trying to find Feael.

Artifact

Feael

Feael is a powerful artifact in the shape of a mushy spear. Gravity pours away from it. It is a pale black color. It smells like root beer. When worshipped it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
