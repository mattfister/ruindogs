Haeua Cfwiideh

Overview

Haeua Cfwiideh is located on a haunted mountain. Some areas of it are flooded. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Pixies. Jimmy Boren The Gullible, a Drow Elite Warrior is here. The Pixies are the slaves of Jimmy Boren The Gullible. He is trying to discover The Secondary Star.

Artifact

The Secondary Star

The Secondary Star looks like a glassy crystal. It is a medium pink color. When carried it destroys itself.
