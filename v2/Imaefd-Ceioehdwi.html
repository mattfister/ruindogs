Overview

Imaefd Ceioehdwi is located in a flooded mountain. Parts of Imaefd Ceioehdwi are flooded. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Yuan-Ti. Rex Magana The Lazy, a Myconid Sovereign is here. The Yuan-Ti are ruled by Rex Magana The Lazy. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Afuiaeum

Afuiaeum has the form of a broken amulet. It is a bright red color. It smells like castoreum. Magic flows towards it. When rubbed it destroys itself.
