Overview

Beael Idaal is located under a volcanic rift. Parts of it are inaccessible. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Githzerai. Rupert Browder The Grumpy, a Cyclops is here. The Githzerai are the soldiers of Rupert Browder The Grumpy. He is trying to research Dedaeamd.

Dedaeamd

Dedaeamd has the form of a transparent amulet. It smells like black currant. Water slips towards it. When gazed upon it destroys others.
