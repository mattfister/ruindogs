The Old Affair

Overview

The Old Affair is located in a haunted mountain. Some rooms of it are cursed. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Drows. Candis Hurley The Quick-Tempered, a Cloud Giant is here. The Drows are the slaves of Candis Hurley The Quick-Tempered. She is trying to exploit Feca.

Artifact

Feca

Feca is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent spear. Air bends from it. When touched it tunnels into the earth.
