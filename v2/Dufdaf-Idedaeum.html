Overview

Dufdaf Idedaeum is located under a giant rift. Some areas of it are flooded. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Sahuagin. Russ Mclemore The Callous, a Myconid Sovereign is here. The Sahuagin are the minions of Russ Mclemore The Callous. He is founding a new religion.

Artifact

Dad

Dad is a powerful artifact in the shape of a glassy rock. Power pours towards it. It is a bright blue color. When held it floats in the air.
