Overview

Dudohedaeum Idaeda is located under a giant rift. Regions of Dudohedaeum Idaeda are flooded. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Kobolds. Edra Gillespie The Careless, a Druid is here. The Kobolds are the minions of Edra Gillespie The Careless. She is founding a new religion.
