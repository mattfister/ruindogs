The Free Garage

Overview

The Free Garage is constructed on a haunted mountain. Some rooms of The Free Garage are flooded. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Sprites. Lou Metz The Boastful, an Assassin is here. The Sprites are the minions of Lou Metz The Boastful. He is trying to use Gus.

Artifact

Gus

Gus is a powerful artifact in the shape of a warm cube. Power bends near it. When worn it sings the hymn of the damned.
