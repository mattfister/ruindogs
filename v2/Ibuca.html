Overview

Ibuca is located under an alien mountain. Some rooms of Ibuca are incredibly cold. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Sahuagins. Dulcie Dockery The Stupid, a Sahuagin Baron is here. The Sahuagins are the minions of Dulcie Dockery The Stupid. She is founding a new religion.

The Strategic Dog

The Strategic Dog is a powerful artifact in the shape of a wet orb. Air shifts around it. When rubbed it glows with an eerie light.
