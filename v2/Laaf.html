Overview

Laaf is constructed on a haunted plain. Some rooms of it are flooded. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Drows. Tyler Tellez The Dishonest, a Drow Priestess of Lolth is here. The Drows are the minions of Tyler Tellez The Dishonest. She is founding a new religion.

Artifact

The Linear Jam

The Linear Jam looks like an opaque rock. It is a dark purple color. It smells like quince. When eaten it becomes a force of destiny.
