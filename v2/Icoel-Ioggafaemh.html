Overview

Icoel Ioggafaemh is constructed on a ruined city. Regions of it are cursed. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Githyanki. Shawnee Napier The Timid, a Cambion is here. The Githyanki are the minions of Shawnee Napier The Timid. She is trying to exploit Idacaebam.

Idacaebam

Idacaebam has the form of a mushy amulet. It is a dark gray color. It smells like herbs. When carried it ignites its surrroundings.
