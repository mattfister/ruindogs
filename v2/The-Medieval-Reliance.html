The Medieval Reliance

Overview

The Medieval Reliance is located under a crystal tree. Regions of it are corrupted. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Douglass Lavender The Rude, a Kuo-Toa Monitor is here. The Githzerai worship Douglass Lavender The Rude. He is trying to discover The Musical Tribe.

Artifact

The Musical Tribe

The Musical Tribe has the form of a broken orb. When carried it makes its owner invisible.
