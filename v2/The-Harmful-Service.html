The Harmful Service

Overview

The Harmful Service is located in a ruined rift. Some rooms of it are flooded. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Satyrs. Karmen Carlisle The Selfish, a Mind Flayer is here. The Satyrs worship Karmen Carlisle The Selfish. She is trying to understand Daehh Beaemdamemca.

Artifact

Daehh Beaemdamemca

Daehh Beaemdamemca looks like a soft monument. It is a bright gray color. It smells like cassia. When carried it becomes a deadly projectile.
