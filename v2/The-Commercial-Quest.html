The Commercial Quest

Overview

The Commercial Quest is located under a crystal rift. Some areas of The Commercial Quest are foggy. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Dryads. Mac Finch The Conservative, a Drow Priestess of Lolth is here. The Dryads worship Mac Finch The Conservative. He is trying to understand The Basic Deprivation.

Artifact

The Basic Deprivation

The Basic Deprivation has the form of an opaque amulet. Magic pours around it. It is a bright black color. When cradled it makes its owner invisible.
