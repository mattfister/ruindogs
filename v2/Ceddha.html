Ceddha

Overview

Ceddha is located in a ruined mountain. Some areas of it are flooded. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Troglodytes. Felipe Harvey The Moody, a Cloud Giant is here. The Troglodytes are the minions of Felipe Harvey The Moody. He is trying to find Mufaesum.

Artifact

Mufaesum

Mufaesum has the form of a wet cube. It smells like ozone. Water pours away from it. When worn it floats above the ground.
