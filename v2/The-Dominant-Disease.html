Overview

The Dominant Disease is located in a ruined rift. Some rooms of it are unbearably cold. The ruin is coming to life. It is occupied by Cyclopses. Rene Machado The Compulsive, a Frost Giant is here. The Cyclopses worship Rene Machado The Compulsive. He is trying to recover The Friendly Protection.

Artifact

The Friendly Protection

The Friendly Protection has the form of a soft doll. It is a bright gray color. When smelled it repels insects.
