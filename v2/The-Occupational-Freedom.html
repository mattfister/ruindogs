The Occupational Freedom

Overview

The Occupational Freedom is located in a poisoned plain. Some rooms of The Occupational Freedom are inaccessible. A battle between raiders is happening outside. It is occupied by Pixies. Karol Ketchum The Harsh, a Sahuagin Baron is here. The Pixies have been charmed by Karol Ketchum The Harsh. She is trying to research Emehwiiaei.

Artifact

Emehwiiaei

Emehwiiaei looks like a broken gem. It smells like cantaloupe. It is a shifting black color. When eaten it destroys itself.
