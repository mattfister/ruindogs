##Cubdadaedaeum Duma
###Overview
Cubdadaedaeum Duma is located in a poisoned city. Some rooms of it are frozen. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Dryads. <a name="Gus-Marin-The-Greedy"></a>Gus Marin The Greedy, a Fire Giant is here. The Dryads have been charmed by Gus Marin The Greedy. He  is trying to hide [Homh](#Homh). 



###Artifact
####<a name="Homh"></a>Homh


Homh looks like a cold doll. It smells like oats. It is a medium brown color. When gazed upon it grants psychic powers.
