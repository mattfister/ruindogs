Huehcaadaf Cuuafeha is located on a flooded plain. Some areas of it are corrupted. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Thri-Kreens. Vernell Jeter The Weak-Willed, a Cult Fanatic is here. The Thri-Kreens are the soldiers of Vernell Jeter The Weak-Willed. She is trying to destroy Icadcm.
