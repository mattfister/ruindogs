The Hungry Break

Overview

The Hungry Break is located under a flooded mountain. Parts of The Hungry Break are flooded. A lunar eclipse is happening outside. It is occupied by Harpies. Dell Lira The Quarrelsome, a Sahuagin Baron is here. The Harpies are the minions of Dell Lira The Quarrelsome. She is trying to understand Cuudafedaeum.

Artifact

Cuudafedaeum

Cuudafedaeum is a powerful artifact in the shape of a broken rock. It is a medium orange color. It smells like acetone. When touched it becomes lost.
