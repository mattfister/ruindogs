Iucaeuhuhwi

Overview

Iucaeuhuhwi is located in a haunted plain. Some areas of it are cursed. The ruin is flooding. It is occupied by Harpies. Ivette Keener The Selfish, a Fire Giant is here. The Harpies have been charmed by Ivette Keener The Selfish. She is trying to recover Ihaed.

Artifact

Ihaed

Ihaed is a powerful artifact in the shape of a glassy gem. It smells like eucalyptus. When touched it becomes a force of destiny.
