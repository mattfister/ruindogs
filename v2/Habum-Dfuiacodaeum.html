Ruin Dogs

Habum Dfuiacodaeum

Overview

Habum Dfuiacodaeum is located on an alien plain. Regions of it are foggy. The ruin is sinking into the earth. It is occupied by Orcs. Cesar Ochs The Thoughtless, a Mind Flayer Arcanist is here. The Orcs worship Cesar Ochs The Thoughtless. He is trying to steal Deddha Babufemlob.

Artifact

Deddha Babufemlob

Deddha Babufemlob is a powerful artifact in the shape of a transparent gem. Cacophony bends around it. When gazed upon it frightens children.
