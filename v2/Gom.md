##Gom
###Overview
Gom is located on a broken city. Some areas of it are flooded. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Drows. <a name="Marlyn-Bouchard-The-Inconsistent"></a>Marlyn Bouchard The Inconsistent, a Priest is here. The Drows are ruled by Marlyn Bouchard The Inconsistent. She  is trying to hide [Foaem Omaedwi](#Foaem-Omaedwi). 



###Artifact
####<a name="Foaem-Omaedwi"></a>Foaem Omaedwi


Foaem Omaedwi looks like a mushy blade. Power incinerates away from it. When picked up it turns surrounding objects to ashes.
