Coaeca Laaf is located in an alien rift. Parts of it are flooded. The ruin is larger on the inside than the outside. It is occupied by Kuo-Toa. Kenisha Mathis The Cowardly, a Spined Devil is here. The Kuo-Toa are battling Kenisha Mathis The Cowardly. She is trying to find The Genuine Publisher.
