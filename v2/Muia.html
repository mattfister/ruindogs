Muia

Overview

Muia is located under an alien city. Some areas of it are inaccessible. The ruin is collapsing slowly. It is occupied by Sahuagin. Willard Boyce The Interfering, a Druid is here. The Sahuagin have been charmed by Willard Boyce The Interfering. He is trying to exploit The Memorable Resort.

Artifact

The Memorable Resort

The Memorable Resort has the form of a sharp gem. Power incinerates away from it. When rubbed it becomes a force of destiny.
