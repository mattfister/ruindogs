Overview

The Helpless Bus is constructed on a volcanic city. Parts of The Helpless Bus are corrupted. A windstorm is happening outside. It is occupied by Githyanki. Les Brady The Clinging, a Hill Giant is here. The Githyanki are the soldiers of Les Brady The Clinging. He is trying to use The Uneasy Result.

Artifact

The Uneasy Result

The Uneasy Result has the form of a smooth crystal. It is a pale red color. It smells like privet. When eaten it changes the past.
