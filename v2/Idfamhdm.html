Overview

Idfamhdm is located under a broken city. Some rooms of Idfamhdm are incredibly cold. A massive storm is happening outside. It is occupied by Duergars. Alfonso Roman The Nasty, a Lizardfolk Shaman is here. The Duergars are the soldiers of Alfonso Roman The Nasty. He is trying to understand Aemdahfedaeum Heda.

Artifact

Aemdahfedaeum Heda

Aemdahfedaeum Heda is a powerful artifact in the shape of a smooth spear. When worshipped it levitates surrounding objects.
