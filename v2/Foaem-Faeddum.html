Overview

Foaem Faeddum is located in a spikey city. Parts of it are cursed. A blizzard is happening outside. It is occupied by Githzerai. Ollie Melendez The Sneaky, a Yuan-Ti Malison is here. The Githzerai are ruled by Ollie Melendez The Sneaky. He is trying to steal Duhaecwi Gaefa.

Duhaecwi Gaefa has the form of a smooth rock. It smells like acai. Cacophony glows towards it. When cradled it becomes lost.
